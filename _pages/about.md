Hi! 
I am an incoming PhD student at the [School of Automation](https://soa.csu.edu.cn/index.htm), [Central South University](https://en.csu.edu.cn/), supervised by Prof. [Runmin Zou](https://faculty.csu.edu.cn/zourunmin/en/index.htm).
Before this, I obtained a Master's degree from the [College of Electronic Information](https://ei.gxmzu.edu.cn/), [Guangxi Minzu University](http://www.gxun.edu.cn/), majoring in Electronic Information, advised by Prof. [Zhenkun Lu](https://ei.gxmzu.edu.cn/info/1053/3281.htm) and Prof. [Qinghua Huang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=zh-CN&user=cdxgLCYAAAAJ). 
My research focuses on load forecasting, time series prediction, smart healthcare, and developing robust and efficient algorithms driven by practical problems.

I have also worked as a visiting student at [CEC, Jimei University](http://cec.jmu.edu.cn/) (2022, Summer) under the guidance of Prof. [Zhijin Wang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=rKUpBpMAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN) and [SIGS, Tsinghua University](https://www.sigs.tsinghua.edu.cn/) (2023, Summer) under the guidance of Prof. [Qiuwei Wu](https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=zh-CN&user=LLit50EAAAAJ&view_op=list_works), focusing on pandemic prediction and heating demand forecasting, respectively. Additionally, I collaborate closely with Dr. [Yuan Zhao](https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=zh-CN&user=b7r1FDwAAAAJ), and Prof. [Xiufeng Liu](http://xiufengliu.github.io/).
