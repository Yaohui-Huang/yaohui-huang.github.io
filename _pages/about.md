Hi! I am currently a three-year Master Student at majoring in Electronic Information [College of Electronic Information](https://ei.gxmzu.edu.cn/), [Guangxi Minzu University](http://www.gxun.edu.cn/). I received my Bachelor’s degree majoring in Computer Science and Technology from [Chengyi College](http://chengyi.jmu.edu.cn/ssjgy.jsp?wbtreeid=1001), [Jimei University](http://www.jmu.edu.cn/) in 2020.  My research focuses on time series forecasting and the development of robust and efficient algorithms driven by practical problems. Specific applications include energy load forecasting, tracing of regional epidemics, and clinical diabetes glucose monitoring. I am advised by Prof. [Zhenkun Lu](https://ei.gxmzu.edu.cn/info/1053/3281.htm) and collaborate closely with Prof. [Zhijin Wang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=rKUpBpMAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN), Prof. [Xiufeng Liu](http://xiufengliu.github.io/), and Prof. [Qinghua Huang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=zh-CN&user=cdxgLCYAAAAJ).

I also worked as a visiting student at [CEC, Jimei University](http://cec.jmu.edu.cn/) (2022, Summer) under the guidance of Prof. [Zhijin Wang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=rKUpBpMAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN) and [TBSI, Tsinghua University](https://www.sigs.tsinghua.edu.cn/) (2023, Summer) under the guidance of Prof. [Qiuwei Wu](https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=zh-CN&user=LLit50EAAAAJ&view_op=list_works), focusing on pandemic prediction and heating demand forecasting, respectively.
