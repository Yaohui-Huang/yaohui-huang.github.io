About Me

I am a second-year PhD student at the School of Automation, Central South University, under the supervision of Prof. Yun Wang and Prof. Runmin Zou.

Previously, I completed my Master's studies under the guidance of Prof. Zhenkun Lu. During my undergraduate studies, I was mentored by Prof. Zhijin Wang. I have also worked as a visiting student at SIGS, Tsinghua University (2023, Summer), under the supervision of Prof. Qiuwei Wu. Additionally, I collaborate closely with Dr. Yuan Zhao and Prof. Xiufeng Liu.

My research interests include extreme regression, energy forecasting, time series analysis, graph neural networks, and mixture of experts. I am dedicated to developing robust and efficient algorithms that address practical challenges.
