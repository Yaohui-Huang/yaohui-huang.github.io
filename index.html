Hi! I am currently a third-year Master’s student at the College of Electronic Information, Guangxi Minzu University, majoring in Electronic Information, advised by Prof. Zhenkun Lu and Prof. Qinghua Huang. I received my Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Technology from Chengyi College, Jimei University in 2020, advised by Prof. Zhijin Wang. My research focuses on time series forecasting, smart healthcare, and developing robust and efficient algorithms driven by practical problems.

I have also worked as a visiting student at CEC, Jimei University (2022, Summer) under the guidance of Prof. Zhijin Wang and TBSI, Tsinghua University (2023, Summer) under the guidance of Prof. Qiuwei Wu, focusing on pandemic prediction and heating demand forecasting, respectively. Additionally, I collaborate closely with Prof. Qiankun Su and Prof. Xiufeng Liu.
